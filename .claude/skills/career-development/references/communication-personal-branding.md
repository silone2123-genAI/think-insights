Communication & Personal Branding (The Visibility)
Highlights how to project competence and build a reputable brand through first impressions and interpersonal skills.
Key Takeaways:
Warmth and Competence: In first encounters, people judge you first on warmth (trust) and then on competence (respect)
. Authenticity is key; seeking positive qualities in others naturally makes you appear warmer
.
Simplify to Amplify: Intelligence is associated with clarity of expression
. Overcomplicating language with jargon requires too much brain power for the listener and can make you seem less smart
.
Strategic Visibility: If you are an introvert, focus your energy outwardly on providing value to the audience rather than on your own anxiety
. Use "coffee cup eye contact" and powerful posture to look and feel more authoritative
.
Magic Phrases: Handle insults or passive-aggression by saying, "That’s interesting, tell me more," which shifts the spotlight back to the antagonizer and buys you time to remain calm
.
Inspirational Follow-up Questions:
If your message was so simple a child could understand it, how much more impact would your value have in the boardroom?
What would change if you entered every room focused on how you could be of service rather than how you are being judged?
