Strategic Advancement & Leadership (The Roadmap)
Focuses on career growth, decision-making, and the influence you have on others.
Key Takeaways:
Career Multipliers: Differentiate yourself by combining typical qualifications with unique, marketable skills like public speaking, negotiation, or cross-industry experience
.
The 5-Step Salary Ask: Secure a raise by starting with gratitude, presenting evidence of performance, comparing market value, making the specific ask, and requesting support
.
The Decision Filter: Pass big choices through three questions: Will this help me grow? Is it based on ego (status/titles)? Does it excite me?
.
Servant Leadership: Exceptional leaders make people feel valued by listening deeply, demonstrating empathy, and caring about employees beyond their job titles
.
Conscious Affiliation: You are the average of the people you spend time with
. Audit your circle to ensure they are "watering your potential" rather than projecting their own fears onto you
.
Inspirational Follow-up Questions:
What unique "combination lock" of skills do you possess that makes you irreplaceable in today’s market?
Who in your circle is currently holding the "script" for your life, and do they have the vision required to help you reach your highest potential?
