# When to Use This Skill
Use this skill when the user asks about career growth, promotions, workplace effectiveness, leadership, or professional development.

---

# Pillar 1: Internal Psychological Mastery
Focus: Build self-awareness and control to prevent self-sabotage and develop earned confidence.

- **Self-Regulation ("Name it to Tame it")**: Identify and label negative emotions or inner criticism to reduce reactivity and restore rational thinking.
- **5 Inner Critic Archetypes**: Recognize common internal voices:
  - Classic Judge (perfectionism)
  - Victim (powerlessness)
  - Protector (risk-aversion)
  - Ringmaster (overwork)
  - Neglector (people-pleasing)
- **Confidence Bank**: Track concrete achievements and positive feedback to counter imposter syndrome and build evidence-based confidence.
- **"Good Enough" Protocol**: Prioritize progress over perfection. Apply strategic imperfection to low-impact tasks to preserve energy.

---

# Pillar 2: Communication & Interpersonal Influence
Focus: Build trust, credibility, and influence through clear and intentional communication.

- **Warmth and Competence (WC)**: Establish trust first (warmth), then demonstrate expertise (competence).
- **Linguistic Clarity**: Use simple, direct language. Clear communication signals intelligence more than jargon.
- **Conflict Diffusion Phrases**:
  - "That’s interesting, tell me more."
  - "What made you say that?"
- **Visual Confidence Cues**:
  - Strong posture to project authority and reduce stress.
  - Maintain eye contact for 2–3 seconds to signal confidence.
- **Strategic Pausing (3SP)**: Pause for ~3 seconds before responding to appear thoughtful and composed.

---

# Pillar 3: Strategic Career Advancement
Focus: Maximize career growth through leverage, positioning, and intentional decisions.

- **Career Multipliers**: Combine your core expertise with high-leverage skills (e.g., communication, negotiation, writing).
- **5-Step Salary Ask Framework**:
  1. Express gratitude.
  2. Present evidence of performance.
  3. Share market comparisons.
  4. Make a clear, specific ask.
  5. Ask for guidance or support.
- **3-Question Decision Filter**:
  - Will this help me grow?
  - Am I choosing this based on ego (status/approval)?
  - Does this genuinely excite me?
- **Servant Leadership**: Elevate others through empathy, active listening, and recognizing individuals beyond their roles.

---

# Pillar 4: Operational Resilience & Focus
Focus: Protect attention, sustain energy, and maintain consistent execution.

- **Conscious Affiliation**: Surround yourself with people who support growth and challenge limiting beliefs.
- **Cognitive Appraisal**: Reframe challenges as opportunities to improve performance and reduce stress.
- **Action-Motivation Loop**: Start with small actions to generate momentum and motivation.
- **Attention Management**: Design your environment for focus:
  - Reduce distractions (e.g., hide phone).
  - Create conditions for deep work.
